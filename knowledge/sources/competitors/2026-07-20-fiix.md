# Fiix

> File under `knowledge/sources/`. Schema: `knowledge/reference/knowledge-schema.md`.

## Snapshot

Cloud CMMS under Rockwell Automation / FactoryTalk Maintenance Suite. Emphasizes asset hierarchies, work orders/requests, analytics, multi-site enterprise, Integration Hub, and AI / Asset Risk Predictor for predictive maintenance.

## Facts checked

- **Rockwell product page:** Fiix as AI-powered CMMS; asset management, reporting/KPIs, work-order/request automation; “3000+ companies” claim on page. Source: https://www.rockwellautomation.com/en-us/products/software/factorytalk/maintenancesuite/fiix.html
- **Enterprise page:** multi-site, currencies/languages, e-signatures, Integration Hub, IoT partners, ARP. Source: https://fiixsoftware.com/enterprise/
- **Industrial integration:** Rockwell messaging ties Fiix to OT (PLC/SCADA/MES) for condition-based work — enterprise plant narrative, not field-supervisor command center.

## Strengths / gaps vs TrueCrew

- **Strong:** enterprise + OT credibility via Rockwell; predictive/ARP story; multi-site governance features (e-sign, SSO-class enterprise asks).
- **Gap:** heavy plant/EAM gravity. TrueCrew ICP (supervisor on a job site) is underserved if the product assumes FactoryTalk-class stack.

## Implications for TrueCrew

Use Fiix as the “enterprise CMMS ceiling.” TrueCrew should stay complementary: command and exception triage, not Maximo/Fiix replacement.

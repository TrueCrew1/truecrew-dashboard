# Limble

> File under `knowledge/sources/`. Schema: `knowledge/reference/knowledge-schema.md`.

## Snapshot

Modern-UI CMMS aimed at mid-market plant/facilities teams that want fast adoption after legacy CMMS pain. Work orders, PM, assets, mobile; higher tiers add inventory, offline, API, multi-site.

## Facts checked

- **Vendor cost page:** lists Limble Standard at $28/user/month (among peer CMMS prices). Plans described as Standard / Premium+ / Enterprise. Source: https://limble.com/learn/cost
- **Third-party 2026 write-ups (Facilio, Zendikt):** mid-market positioning; rapid deploy; mobile-first; Standard ~$28; Premium+ higher with offline/parts/API; Enterprise custom (SSO, multi-location, approval workflows). Treat third-party numbers as **reported**, re-check calculator before any pricing claim in sales copy.

## Strengths / gaps vs TrueCrew

- **Strong:** simplicity + mid-market clarity; PM compliance reporting called out by analysts as audit-friendly (marketing/analyst claim).
- **Gap:** still a CMMS execution system. TrueCrew’s edge is ops command for supervisors, not replacing Limble’s asset hierarchy.

## Implications for TrueCrew

Limble is the “easy CMMS” peer. Do not out-feature Limble; out-focus on supervisor next-action and regulated-ops awareness without claiming to be the CMMS of record.

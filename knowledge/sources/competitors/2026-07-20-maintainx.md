# MaintainX

> File under `knowledge/sources/`. Schema: `knowledge/reference/knowledge-schema.md`.

## Snapshot

Mobile-first CMMS / light EAM for frontline maintenance: work orders, repeating PMs, procedures, messaging on the work order, parts/PO on higher tiers, multi-site on Enterprise. Positions for technicians on phones, not desk admins.

## Facts checked

- **Pricing (vendor page, 2026-07-20):** Basic free; Essential $20/user/mo annual ($25 monthly); Premium $65/$75; Enterprise custom. Requester accounts free. Source: https://www.getmaintainx.com/pricing
- **Core claims on same page:** unlimited work orders (Basic+), real-time messaging, repeating WOs, offline mode, QR/barcode, procedures/signatures, meter-based maintenance (Premium+), parts/PO (Premium+), SSO/multi-site (Enterprise).
- **Enterprise marketing:** “maintenance, safety, and operational excellence” — compliance language is marketing, not a named regulator program.

## Strengths / gaps vs TrueCrew

- **Strong:** phone-native WO lifecycle; free requesters; published pricing; procedure + messaging baked into WO.
- **Gap for TrueCrew angle:** sells *execution CMMS*, not a supervisor command center (Today → Ops → Review). No evidence of MSHA/OSHA/DOT-specific surfaces from public pages reviewed.

## Implications for TrueCrew

Compete on “what do I do next” for supervisors, not on feature parity with full CMMS. Treat MaintainX as the mobile-WO bar TrueCrew must not undercut when linking field closeout.

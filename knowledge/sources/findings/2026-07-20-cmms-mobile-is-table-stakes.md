# CMMS mobile is table stakes

> File under `knowledge/sources/`. Schema: `knowledge/reference/knowledge-schema.md`. Not policy until validated/promoted.

## Context

Synthesized from public competitor profiles filed the same day (MaintainX, UpKeep, Limble, Fiix).

## Finding

Every reviewed CMMS competitor **markets mobile work-order execution** (create/update/close on phone, often with offline) as core, not optional.

## Evidence

- MaintainX: mobile-first positioning; offline on pricing feature matrix.
- UpKeep: “mobile-first CMMS”; dedicated mobile/offline help.
- Limble: mobile app called out in mid-market reviews and vendor messaging.
- Fiix: desktop **and** mobile apps on enterprise page.

## Implications for TrueCrew

Do not pitch “we have a mobile app for closing WOs” as differentiation. Differentiate on **supervisor command / next action** while assuming mobile closeout exists elsewhere.

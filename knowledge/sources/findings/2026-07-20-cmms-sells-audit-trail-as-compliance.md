# CMMS sells audit trail as compliance

> File under `knowledge/sources/`. Schema: `knowledge/reference/knowledge-schema.md`. Not policy until validated/promoted.

## Context

Competitive positioning from public marketing — **not** an interpretation of OSHA/MSHA/DOT obligations.

## Finding

Incumbent CMMS/EHS suites sell **audit trails, procedures, e-signatures, and PM compliance reporting** as “compliance / safety / operational excellence” capabilities, usually without naming a specific regulator on the homepage.

## Evidence

- UpKeep product page: EHS module with “audit trails,” incidents, inspections, corrective actions.
- MaintainX Enterprise: “maintenance, safety, and operational excellence”; procedures with signature capture on pricing matrix.
- Fiix enterprise: e-signatures; Rockwell messaging on audits/reporting ease.

## Implications for TrueCrew

Expect buyers to ask “does it help with compliance?” Meaning often = **traceable work history**, not a Form 300 system. TrueCrew should separate **work evidence** from **regulatory recordkeeping** (see OSHA finding).

## Open questions / next actions

- Which buyers mean OSHA 1904 vs MSHA exams vs internal ISO audits when they say “compliance”?

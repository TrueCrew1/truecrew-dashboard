# Supervisor day starts with exceptions

> File under `knowledge/sources/`. Schema: `knowledge/reference/knowledge-schema.md`. Not policy until validated/promoted.

## Context

Hypothesis inferred from CMMS marketing that emphasizes overdue/urgent queues and PM compliance — **not** validated by interviews.

## Finding

**Hypothesis:** field supervisors’ first daily glance is an **exception list** (overdue PMs, urgent WOs, blocked requests), not a full asset registry or analytics dashboard.

## Evidence

- Indirect only: UpKeep markets “urgent, overdue, and preventable”; MaintainX/UpKeep center repeating/overdue WO and PM compliance.
- No interview or ride-along in this corpus.

## Implications for TrueCrew

Design Today around exceptions if interviews confirm; until then, treat as **assumption-driving hypothesis** only.

## Open questions / next actions

- Interview 3+ supervisors: first screen of the day?
- Job-post language scan for “coordinate,” “prioritize,” “backlog” (follow-up pass).
